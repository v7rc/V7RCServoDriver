# Arduino Library Manager — Short Description

## sentence (<= 1 line)
BLE-based V7RC receiver/driver for servos and DC motors (differential & mecanum) on ESP32.

## paragraph (2–4 lines)
Low-latency BLE (NimBLE) receiver compatible with V7RC App (V7RCDOM V2.0).
Maps incoming channels to servos, DC motors, and drive kinematics including Differential Drive and Mecanum.
Includes failsafe timeout and examples for ESP32-C3 robots.

## category
Communication

## architectures
esp32

## depends
ESP32Servo, NimBLE-Arduino
